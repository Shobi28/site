BIOTECHNOLOGY

    OVERVIEW

  • The Department of Biotechnology was established in the year 2002.

  • It offers B.Tech and M.Tech Biotechnology under Anna University, Chennai.

  • The B.Tech Industrial Biotechnology programme was started in the year 2003 with an intake of 30 students/batch.

  • The intake was then increased to 60 in 2007; 60 to 120 in the year 2013 and 120 to 180 in the year 2015.

  • The B.Tech Biotechnology programme was re-accredited by NBA for 6 years during 2017-18 academic year.

  • The 2 year M.Tech. Biotechnology programme was started in 2008-2009.

  • The Department of Biotechnology has been recognized as a collaborative research centre for M.S/PhD programme from 2008 onwards by Anna University, Chennai.

    • VISION

  • To excel in academics and become a pioneer in research by nurturing the students with technical and innovative expertise to connect better with industry and society.

    • MISSION

  • To equip students with knowledge and practical skills relevant to the real life applications.

  • To establish Centre of Excellence in the frontier areas of research.

  • To develop manpower with high level of professionalism.
